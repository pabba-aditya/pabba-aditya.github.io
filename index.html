About

We are an open-source offline first, data collection toolkit, created specifically for low resource environments

Features

tangerine is flexible and customizable, allowing you to adapt the product to your specific project needs

Where we are used

Over 1.5 million assessments and surveys deployed in 60+ countries, in 100+ languages, for 50+ organizations
